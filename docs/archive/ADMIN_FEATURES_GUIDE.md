## 1.     Interactive Stat Cards

### **Clickable Cards**
- **Total Hackathons**: Click to view the full list of all hackathons (Pending, Accepted, Declined).
- **Pending**: Click to view only Pending hackathons.
- **Accepted**: Click to view only Accepted hackathons.
- **Total Students**: Click to go to User Management.

### **Expandable Cards**
- Click the **‚ñº arrow** on "Total Hackathons" or "Pending" cards.
- Shows a **quick preview** of the latest items without leaving the page.
- Click "View All" inside the expanded card to go to the full list.

---

## 2.   Enhanced User Management

### **Assign Proctors**
- You can now **assign a proctor** to any student directly from the dashboard.
- **How:**
  1. Go to "User Management" tab.
  2. Click "  Edit" on a student.
  3. Select a proctor from the "Assign Proctor" dropdown.
  4. Click "Update".

### **Filter by Proctor**
- View students assigned to a specific proctor.
- **How:**
  1. Go to "User Management" tab.
  2. Use the **"Filter by Proctor"** dropdown at the top.
  3. Select a proctor to see only their students.

### **Proctor Column**
- The student table now shows the **assigned proctor's name**.

---

## 3.    Hackathon Management Tab

### **New Dedicated Tab**
- A new **"   Hackathons"** tab has been added.
- View **ALL** hackathons (not just accepted ones).
- **Filter** by status: Pending, Accepted, Declined.
- See details: Title, Student, Date, Status, Assigned Proctor.

---

## 4. üîÑ Automatic Synchronization

- When you assign a proctor to a student:
  - The student is updated with the new proctor.
  - The student is automatically added to the proctor's "Assigned Students" list.
  - If the student had a previous proctor, they are removed from that proctor's list.
